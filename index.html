Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept insurance or HSA payments?
Eclipse Spa operates on a private-pay basis. We do not direct-bill insurance for cosmetic or wellness treatments. However, many massage therapy services may be eligible for reimbursement through your health benefits. We accept all major credit cards, debit, and e-transfer.
What is your cancellation policy?
We kindly ask for 24 hours notice for any cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a service fee. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule.
Is there parking at the spa?
Yes! Eclipse Spa offers complimentary parking for all guests directly outside our location. We are also accessible via TTC — please ask our team for the nearest transit options.
What should I do to prepare for my appointment?
We recommend arriving 10–15 minutes early to complete your intake form and settle in. For facial appointments, arrive with a clean face if possible. For massage, wear comfortable clothing.
Do you offer gift cards?
Yes, we offer digital gift cards — the perfect gift for loved ones. We also offer curated treatment packages at special rates. Contact us at eclipsespa.ca@gmail.com or ask our reception for current offerings.
